Liver Disease Awareness & Prediction

Blood Donor

A healthy blood donor shows no significant liver abnormalities. Regular checkups are recommended to maintain overall liver health.

Suspect Blood Donor

A suspect blood donor may have minor liver irregularities or borderline test results. Further testing is recommended before donation.

Hepatitis

Hepatitis is an inflammation of the liver, often caused by viral infection, alcohol, or toxins. Early detection and treatment can prevent severe complications.

Fibrosis

Fibrosis is the formation of excess connective tissue in the liver due to chronic damage. It can progress to cirrhosis if untreated.

Cirrhosis

Cirrhosis is severe liver scarring from long-term damage. It can lead to liver failure. Regular monitoring and lifestyle changes are crucial for management.
